Valorant


Valorant is currently the only video game I play since Fortnite & Warzone got boring. Valorant is similar to CS:GO, both are 5v5 death match game with a bomb which can be planted to put pressure on the opposing team. Valorant however has characters with special abilities which makes the game standout. Valorant rounds are 1:40 seconds long, and when the spike is planted, the game has only 40 seconds left. A full game is played to 13 rounds, and the teams switch from offense to defense at 12 rounds.
